One of the ways to measure the health of the current podcast ecosystem is to measure
_new episodes published_.

We've been looking at _every single new podcast episode published_ and identifying which
podcast hosting company it belongs to, primarily by following the media file url past
any tracking redirects.

There are many market share numbers floating around out there for the various podcast hosting companies, 
but it's often difficult to compare apples to apples - especially when considering dead shows.
Looking at only new episodes is one fairly objective way to compare each platform, at
least by sheer amount of new output.

Below is the ranking of podcast hosters ordered by episodes published in September 2021.

A rounded percentage of episode share is also included if > 0%.
